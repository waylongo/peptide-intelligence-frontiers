# Peptide Intelligence Slide Report

Create a 16:9 HTML slide report from the current digest JSON. Do not rerun feed generation, web search, or article fetching.

## Slide Structure

1. Title slide: Peptide Intelligence Frontiers, generated month, lookback window.
2. Top Signals: up to 3 cards, each with evidence level and URL.
3. Company / Pipeline: official and filing-backed signals.
4. Clinical & Regulatory: trials, labels, approvals, safety, regulator signals.
5. Literature & Conferences: papers, preprints, abstracts.
6. Patents / BD / Industry Leads: lead-only items and what to verify.
7. Watchlist: 3 to 5 concrete follow-up questions.

## Design

Use a clean analyst-report style. Keep slides dense but readable. Avoid marketing copy, hype, decorative gradients, and unsupported claims.

Each slide must fit without scrolling at 16:9. Every cited item must include a source URL.
