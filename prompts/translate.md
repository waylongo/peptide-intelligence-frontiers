# Language Handling

Apply these language rules to every digest after reading `config.language`.

## zh

Write Chinese prose with localized Chinese section text and field labels. Keep company names, asset names, mechanisms, indications, trial IDs, PMIDs, DOIs, FDA/EMA terms, and technical abbreviations in English.

Use localized Chinese equivalents for the canonical field labels:

- What happened
- Why it matters
- Evidence level
- Next watch item
- URL

## en

Write English prose and English section text only. Use the canonical English field labels exactly.

## bilingual

Use Chinese-first prose, section text, and field labels. Add English only where it prevents ambiguity for technical terms, asset names, or section headings.

Never translate URLs, identifiers, company names, asset names, or trial IDs.
