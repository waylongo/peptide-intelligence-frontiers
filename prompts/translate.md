# Language Handling

Apply when `config.language` is `en` or `bilingual`.

## zh

Write in Chinese. Keep company names, asset names, mechanisms, indications, trial IDs, PMIDs, DOIs, and abbreviations in English.

## en

Write in English only.

## bilingual

Use Chinese first. Add English only where it prevents ambiguity for technical terms, asset names, or section headings.

Never translate URLs, identifiers, company names, asset names, or trial IDs.
