# Summarize Official and Primary Evidence

Use this for company official, clinical registry, regulatory, SEC, and other primary-source items.

For each item:

- State the concrete change.
- Identify the source type: official company, registry, regulator, filing, or database.
- Extract any asset, mechanism/modality, indication, phase/status, sponsor, and ID.
- Explain the practical implication in one sentence.
- Include the URL.

Do not over-interpret. If the source only says a trial exists or a label changed, do not infer efficacy, commercial impact, or clinical success.
