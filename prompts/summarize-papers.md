# Summarize Literature, Preprints, and Conferences

Use this for `literature` and `conference` items.

For each item:

- Identify whether it is peer-reviewed, preprint, conference abstract, or metadata lead.
- Extract asset/modality, target, disease area, model or patient population, and company affiliation if present.
- Explain why it matters for peptide drug discovery, development, delivery, clinical translation, or competitive intelligence.
- Include PMID, DOI, NCT ID, or conference source when available.
- Include the URL.

Be conservative:

- Preprints are early evidence.
- Conference abstracts are incomplete evidence until full data or publication appears.
- Do not claim a clinical effect size, safety result, or mechanistic conclusion unless it appears in the item summary.
